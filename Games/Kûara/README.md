##  About The Project

<div align="center">
  <img src="https://github.com/bpsoraggi/Personal-Projects/blob/main/Games/K%C3%BBara/imgs/player.gif" alt="gif" width="50%" height="50%">
</div>

K没ara (possible temporary name) will be a crafting top-down game. I plan on having the story element be a very prominent feature, but not the only thing about the game. The player will be able to follow the lore through cutscenes, perhaps a few story choices along the way, and also through visual hints scattered through the map. Once the story is "complete", the game will still be playable and the player will still be able to craft and expand their house/village, etc., similar to how you can still play Minecraft even after defeating the Ender Dragon.

I will keep a few devlogs here to track my progress, but I plan on not exposing too much of the game, especially not story-wise.
